Privacy Policy

At JobTrust Connect, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, services, and applications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, resume/CV, and other details you provide when registering, applying for jobs, or contacting us. We may also collect non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and IP address for analytics and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect you with potential employers and job opportunities.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To send you relevant updates, alerts, and promotional materials (only with your consent).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners, recruiters, or service providers for the purpose of delivering our services.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and display relevant ads. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Our site may contain links to third-party websites, including advertisers and job boards. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

6. Security

We implement strong technical and administrative measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated date will be shown at the top of this page, and significant changes will be communicated through our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@jobtrustconnect.com.
