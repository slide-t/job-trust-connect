About Us ▼

We are a new company from Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State. Started by two people, we aim to provide trustworthy job opportunities. Our mission includes helping the less privileged and connecting people to great jobs.

Our Services ▼

EduKids Africa (since 2019): Educating children across Africa.

SmartKids Coding (since 2022): Introducing coding to children and teens.

JobTrust Connect (since 2025): Bridging the gap between job seekers and opportunities.
