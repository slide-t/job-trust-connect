JobTrust Connect - Company Policies

At JobTrust Connect, we are committed to creating a safe, professional, and productive environment for our staff, clients, and job seekers. These policies outline our values, standards, and expectations.

1. Commitment to Professionalism

  • All staff and clients must maintain the highest level of integrity in all dealings.
  • Harassment, discrimination, or unethical practices will not be tolerated.
  • Confidentiality of client and staff information is a top priority.

2. Workplace Safety & Healthy Living

  • We encourage staff to maintain a healthy work-life balance.
  • Healthy living programs, wellness workshops, and regular breaks are part of our company culture.
  • Employees are expected to respect workplace health guidelines for the safety of all.

3. Equal Opportunity & Diversity

  • JobTrust Connect provides equal opportunity to all, regardless of gender, religion, or background.
  • Diversity and inclusion are core strengths that we actively promote.

4. Client & Job Seeker Relationship

  • All parties must avoid relationships that could compromise trust or lead to a bridge of our company policies.
  • Transparency in contracts and communication is expected at all times.

5. Technology & Data Use

  • Company technology and data should be used responsibly and securely.
  • Misuse of digital tools or unauthorized data sharing is prohibited.

6. Disciplinary Measures

  • Failure to comply with these policies may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or termination of contract.

By engaging with JobTrust Connect, you agree to abide by these policies in good faith. Together, we build trust and create opportunities.
